COPS (Club Of ProgrammerS), IIT BHU is programming club under Science and Technology Council and is a part of Gymkhana, IIT BHU. This club is responsible for coding and mathematical events and handling coding projects related to artificial intelligence, machine learning, information security, app development, web development in the institute and beyond. Every year, an event from Computer Engineering Society, Codefest and Technex event Byte the Bits is organized by COPS.
